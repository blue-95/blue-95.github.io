ProKN: Biomedical Knowledge Graph

An advanced graph architecture mapping behavioral health phenotypes to molecular layers to accelerate translational research. Disjointed biological databases and high-volume data duplication hindered the discovery of complex pharmacological relationships. Engineered robust ETL pipelines using Python and Neo4j, integrating over 20,000 drug and protein entities across Open Targets, ChEMBL, and UniProt. Implemented deterministic UUID v5 hashing for zero-duplication entity resolution. Discovered approximately 10,000 novel drug-protein mechanisms, significantly expanding biological coverage for clinical insights.
